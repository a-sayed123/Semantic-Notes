* section is a branched content meaning .
* aside is an independent related content .
--------------------------------------------------
-- SECTION
--------
• Part of the main content flow.

• The user needs it to understand the primary topic.

• Deleting it removes part of the content itself.

-- Examples:
- Features section
- Hourly forecast
- Comments section
- Product specifications

-- ASIDE
------
• Related but secondary content.

• Useful but not required to understand the main content.

• Deleting it does not break the primary content.

Examples:
- Related articles
- Sidebar navigation
- Author bio
- Advertisements
- Extra recommendations

-- Golden Rule:
• If removing it harms understanding → section.
• If removing it only removes extra context → aside.
