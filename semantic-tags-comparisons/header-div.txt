
header-vs-div

<header> is not valuable because of styling.

<header> is valuable because it identifies the introductory content
of a page, article, section, or other sectioning content.

Outside sectioning content:
→ acts as a banner landmark.

Inside sectioning content:
→ loses banner landmark semantics
→ keeps structural semantics.

Use div when you only need a container.

Use header when the content represents the introduction
or metadata of a section/page/article.
